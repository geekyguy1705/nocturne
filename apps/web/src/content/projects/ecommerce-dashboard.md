A fast, static-first dashboard for online merchants. The dashboard surfaces sales trends, low-stock alerts, and recent orders while keeping the landing experience lightweight.

## Highlights

- Static marketing pages with Astro
- Interactive charts rendered as React islands
- Serverless inventory sync with Supabase
- Dark mode support out of the box

## Outcome

The marketing pages score 99 on Lighthouse performance while the merchant dashboard stays responsive with 100k+ SKUs.
